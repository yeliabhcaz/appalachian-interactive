Our Process

01

Client Needs Analysis

We will start by getting to know you and your business. What are you hoping to get out of your website? What online tools are you already leveraging? Are you more of a DIYer? Or do you want us to handle the load?

02

Digital Audit

Next we will walk through your current online tools to get an idea of our starting point.

03

Planning Phase

Once we have the jumping off point our team will build out a few custom options to present to your business.

04

Client Strategy Meeting

At this meeting we will explain the pros and cons of each option to you. By the end of this meeting we will have our roadmap for your new website!

05

Development

This is the phase where our team works their magic on making your website come to life!

06

Maintenance

Once your website is up and running the fun really starts. Ask about our Google Business management, SEO, Email Marketing, and Facebook management services designed to continue your businesses momentum!
