Our Process

01

Client Needs Analysis and Digital Audit

We will start by getting to know you and your business. What are you hoping to get out of your website? What online tools are you already leveraging? Are you more of a DIYer? Or do you want us to handle the load?

02

Planning Phase

Once we have a jumping off point it's time to get to the drawing board. Our team will research and build out a few solutions to propose to you at our next meeting.

03

Client Strategy Meeting

Next we will convene to go over the proposed solutions. Collaboration is crucial in this step, even if we had all the technical expertise in the world, no one understands your clients and your business better than you. By the end of this meeting we will have a framework to begin developing your new website!

04

Development

We use an agile development process that involves progress checks at predefined checkpoints. This allows you to provide feedback as the website transitions from theory to reality and ensure it stays aligned with your vision.

05

Maintenance

Your website isn't a "set it and forget it" tool, it's a living resource for your company and should be maintained as so. We offer training for people who want to handle it themselves, or service packages if you'd like us to take it off your to do list.
